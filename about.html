Our Story

We built the bottle we wished existed.

After years of forgetting to drink water, feeling the symptoms of dehydration, and never really knowing how to drink more water — we designed HYDRA. A quiet, beautiful water reminder that makes staying hydrated the easiest healthy habit you'll ever build.

A bottle that understands you.

HYDRA calculates your daily water intake based on your body, activity, and climate. Whether you're staying hydrated at work or staying hydrated at the gym, gentle reminders keep you on track — no willpower required.

Hydration is health.

Dehydration slows metabolism, fogs your thinking, and stalls water and weight loss goals. HYDRA is designed to prevent the symptoms of dehydration before they start — even when you're staying hydrated in hot weather.
